About the Formats

SRT (SubRip): The most common subtitle format. Simple and compatible with almost all video players.

VTT (WebVTT): The standard for web videos (HTML5). Very similar to SRT but with extended capabilities for the web.

ASS/SSA (Advanced SubStation Alpha): An advanced format capable of complex styling, including colors, fonts, and positioning. The "Remove styling" option will strip this data, extracting only the plain text.

TXT (Text): A plain text output containing only the dialogue, with no timestamps or formatting.
